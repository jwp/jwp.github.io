Gingerbread

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter.

  • 1/2 cup white sugar.

  • 1 large egg.

  • 1 cup molasses.

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda.

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon.

  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger.

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves.

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  • 1 cup hot water.
